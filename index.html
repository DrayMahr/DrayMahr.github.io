About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Information Systems at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen). My research lies at the computational design science, deep learning algorithm, and computational social science, with a special focus on applying AI techniques to business and emergency management contexts. I aim to develop interpretable and practically impactful computational frameworks that can improve decision-making in dynamic and uncertain environments.

Research Interests

Deep Learning Algorithms

Developing and applying advanced deep learning techniques to solve complex business problems, with a focus on interpretability and practical implementation.

Computational Social Science

Leveraging computational methods to analyze social phenomena, particularly in the context of evolving social dynamics.

Emergency Management

Creating AI-driven frameworks for disaster response and management, with emphasis on social mobility patterns during crises.
